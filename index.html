Our story

Roasted close to home.

Gufa Coffee started in 2021 as a single roaster in a Thamel basement. We work directly with three smallholder farms in Gulmi and Ilam, paying above market rate for beans most export roasters overlook.

Every cup here is brewed to order — no batch pots, no shortcuts. If a table's empty when you walk in, that's the point.
